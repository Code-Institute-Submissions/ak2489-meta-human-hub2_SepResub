About Me

My name is Alexander Green, I have been a qualified PT/Coach for over 5 years, I have worked out of commercial and private gyms all over the Denton area. Over the years this has helped me acquire a lot of knowledge from differnt people within the gyms. My goal is to get every client I have to where they want to be whether it be dropping/gaining weight, toning up certain areas of the body or Improving mobility/strength.

I will not sell you dreams, and tell you its going to be easy. But, I will dedicate our time together to make sure you understand what's needed from both myself and you the client to get to your goal.
